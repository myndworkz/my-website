Designing for Freedom

A project in rational anarchism and political design

This page is the beginning of a space dedicated to thoughtful, radical political design. It brings together slogans, commentary, and eventually a collaborative constitution project grounded in anarchist principles.

Drawing on the work of David Graeber, Peter Kropotkin, and contemporary system designers, we aim to create not a utopia, but a toolset for building freedom.
