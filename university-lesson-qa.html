First Day at University

Question and Answer Sheet

Questions

  1. Who are the three people in the conversation, and how are they introduced?
  2. What problem did Malik face on his first day?
  3. Where is Amina from, and what does she study?
  4. What does Malik study, and what does he like doing?
  5. What did Amina do in the morning, and what class was it?
  6. What clubs do Malik, Amina, and Sophia want to join?
  7. What event does Sophia invite them to attend?

Answers

1. The three people are Sophia, Malik, and Amina. Sophia introduces Malik to Amina, and they greet each other politely.

2. Malik got lost on his first day because the university is very big.

3. Amina is from Kenya, and she studies Business.

4. Malik studies computers. He likes technology and makes websites.

5. Amina attended an English class in the morning.

6. Malik wants to join the Tech Club. Amina wants to join the Reading Club. Sophia is in the Drama Club.

7. Sophia invites them to a Drama Club meeting happening that night.
