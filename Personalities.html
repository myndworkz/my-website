Personalities

Welcome to the "Personalities" lesson. This lesson focuses on exploring different personality traits and how to use them. Below, you'll find a presentation to guide you through the key concepts and activities.

Activities

  • Watch the presentation and take notes on the personality traits discussed.
  • Complete the worksheet provided during class to match traits with descriptions.
  • Discuss with a partner: Which traits best describe you? Why?
