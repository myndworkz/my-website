Lesson Portal: First Day at University

Audio Lesson

Script

This lesson explores conversations and vocabulary related to the first day at university, focusing on greetings, introductions, and common phrases used in academic settings.

Sample Script:

Anna: "Hi, I’m Anna! I’m in my first year studying Biology. What about you?"
James: "Nice to meet you, Anna. I’m James. I’m studying Engineering."
Anna: "Are you enjoying your classes so far?"
James: "Yes, but some of the lectures are quite challenging."

Questions About the Audio

  1. What is Anna studying?
  2. How does James feel about his classes?
  3. What question does Anna ask James?
  4. What is James’s field of study?
  5. How do Anna and James introduce themselves?
  6. What phrases are used to describe academic life?
  7. What is the tone of the conversation?

Phrases to Use in Conversation

  • "Hi, I’m [Name]! I’m studying [Subject]."
  • "Nice to meet you."
  • "Are you enjoying your classes so far?"
  • "The lectures are quite challenging."
  • "What about you?"

Pronunciation and Vocabulary Guidance

Key Pronunciation Tips:

  • Practice the rhythm of greetings: "Hi, I’m Anna!" /haɪ aɪm ˈænə/
  • Emphasize linking sounds in "Nice to meet you" /naɪs tə miːt jʊ/.

Vocabulary Focus:

  • Challenging: Something difficult but rewarding.
  • Enjoying: Feeling happy or satisfied with something.
  • Lectures: Formal talks given by professors.
