First Day at University

Comprehension Questions

  1. Who are the three people in the conversation, and how are they introduced?
  2. What problem did Malik face on his first day?
  3. Where is Amina from, and what does she study?
  4. What does Malik study, and what does he like doing?
  5. What did Amina do in the morning, and what class was it?
  6. What clubs do Malik, Amina, and Sophia want to join?
  7. What event does Sophia invite them to attend?
