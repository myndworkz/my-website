First Day at University

Audio Lesson

Comprehension Questions

  1. Who are the three people in the conversation, and how are they introduced?
  2. What problem did Malik face on his first day?
  3. Where is Amina from, and what does she study?
  4. What does Malik study, and what does he like doing?
  5. What did Amina do in the morning, and what class was it?
  6. What clubs do Malik, Amina, and Sophia want to join?
  7. What event does Sophia invite them to attend?

Phrases to Use in Conversation

  • "Hi, [Name]! Nice to meet you."
  • "Where are you from?"
  • "What do you study?"
  • "Did you go to a class today?"
  • "You should come to the meeting tonight!"

Pronunciation and Vocabulary Guidance

Key Pronunciation Tips:

  • Practice "Nice to meet you" /naɪs tə ˈmiːt ju/ with linking sounds.
  • Emphasize rhythm in questions like "What do you study?" /wʌt də ju ˈstʌd.i/.

Vocabulary Focus:

  • Lost: Unable to find your way.
  • Drama club: A group for acting and performing plays.
  • Tech club: A group focused on technology-related activities.
